Who We Are

Faith-Driven Impact for a Sustainable South Sudan

Headquartered in Juba, God Speaking to Mankind's Church and Development Agency (GSM-CDA) works at the intersection of faith, environmental justice, and community empowerment.

Through holistic programs in climate resilience, pollution advocacy, public health, education, peacebuilding, and humanitarian aid, we partner with communities to build a future where both people and planet thrive.

Our Story

Rooted in Faith, Committed to Justice

Since our founding in Juba, GSM-CDA has been guided by the conviction that caring for creation and serving the vulnerable are central to Christian discipleship.

Our Journey

GSM-CDA emerged from a deep concern for the environmental and social challenges facing South Sudan. What began as a local church initiative has grown into a respected development agency.

We integrate biblical principles of stewardship, justice, and compassion with evidence-based approaches.

Founded 2015 Juba Headquarters Faith-Based Community-Led

Our Work

Programs for Holistic Transformation

Integrated initiatives addressing environmental, social, and spiritual dimensions of community well-being.

Environmental Protection & Climate Resilience

Addressing pollution from petroleum extraction, promoting sustainable land use, and building community capacity for climate adaptation.

  • Community pollution monitoring
  • Climate-smart agriculture training
  • Reforestation and ecosystem restoration

Public Health Advocacy

Promoting clean water, sanitation, disease prevention, and health education.

  • WASH programs
  • Health education in churches and schools
  • Maternal and child health support
